About us

Software first. Hardware not included.

Aetherworkz is a software company built around application and website development. We focus on reliable code, purposeful interfaces, and stable backends.

Our current expertise includes XAMPP-based web projects, PHP scripting, frontend and backend development, Android Studio web applications, and networking fundamentals from CCNA v1.

We are strictly software developers. We do not offer hardware services or embedded engineering.
